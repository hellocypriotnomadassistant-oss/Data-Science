# Data Expertise and Storytelling

## 1. Finding and Telling Data Stories
Finding and telling stories is a core responsibility.
* **Beyond Technicals:** EDA is not just about coding or statistics; the primary goal is to discover, shape, and share the hidden narratives within data.
* **Holistic Approach:** A significant part of data expertise lies beyond the code, focusing on the meaning behind the numbers.

## 2. Applications and Importance of EDA
* **Core Practices:** Discovering, structuring, cleaning, joining, validating, and presenting.
* **Framework:** These practices serve as a guide while exploring data stories within the **PACE workflow**.
* **Integrity:** Ethics and accurate representation are critical components of data-driven storytelling.

## 3. The Role of Data Visualizations
Visualizations are essential tools for understanding, creating, and presenting stories.
* **Ethical Design:** Ethical and inclusive visualizations convey information without misleading the audience.
* **Impact:** They bridge the gap between complex data and stakeholder understanding.

## 4. Career and Applied Learning
* **Evolution:** As the journey progresses, technical tools (Python, libraries) will be used to bring these hidden stories to the surface.

## 5. Inspiration and Perspective
* **Global Impact:** The data stories you discover have the potential to change companies or even the world.

> **Summary:** Data expertise is built on a foundation that combines technical skills with the ability to derive meaning, maintain ethical standards, and master the art of storytelling. EDA and visualization sit at the heart of this process.
